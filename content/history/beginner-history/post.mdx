# Top 3 Resources To Learn World History, For Beginners

World history is a vast and captivating subject, tracing the narrative of humanity from ancient civilizations to the modern era. For those just starting their journey into the annals of our past, it can be overwhelming to know where to begin. Here are three top resources tailored for beginners eager to explore the rich tapestry of world history.

## 1. **BBC - History for Kids**
[BBC's History for Kids](https://www.bbc.co.uk/history/forkids/) provides a fun and engaging introduction to world history. The platform covers key historical periods, from the Stone Age to World War II, using interactive timelines, games, and quizzes. It's an excellent way for beginners, especially younger learners, to get acquainted with major historical events and figures.

## 2. **CrashCourse - World History**
[CrashCourse](https://www.youtube.com/user/crashcourse) is a popular YouTube channel known for its educational content. Their [World History playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLBDA2E52FB1EF80C9) offers a series of videos that delve into various historical eras and themes. Hosted by John Green, the videos are both informative and entertaining, making them perfect for those new to the subject.

## 3. **Khan Academy - World History**
[Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/humanities/world-history) offers a comprehensive course on world history. It's structured in a way that's easy to follow, starting from the origins of humanity and progressing through major historical milestones. With video lectures, reading materials, and quizzes, Khan Academy provides a solid foundation for those eager to delve deeper into history.

**Conclusion**  
The story of our world is both intricate and fascinating. With these beginner-friendly resources, you're well on your way to discovering the events, cultures, and figures that have shaped the course of history. Embark on this enlightening journey and witness the unfolding of humanity's shared saga.
