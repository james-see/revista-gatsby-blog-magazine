# Top 3 Resources to Learn How to Draw for Free

Drawing is a timeless skill that anyone can learn with practice and dedication. Whether you're an aspiring artist or just looking to pick up a new hobby, there are countless resources available online to help you get started. Let's dive into the top three free resources that will set you on the path to becoming a skilled artist.

## 1. **Drawspace**
[Drawspace](https://www.drawspace.com/) offers a comprehensive collection of drawing lessons suitable for both beginners and more advanced artists. With courses curated by professional artists, you'll find tutorials on everything from basic shapes and shading to intricate portrait drawing. The best part? It's completely free!

## 2. **YouTube - Drawing Tutorials**
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/) is a goldmine for free drawing tutorials. Channels like [Proko](https://www.youtube.com/user/ProkoTV) and [Draw With Jazza](https://www.youtube.com/user/DrawWithJazza) offer high-quality lessons that cover a wide range of topics. Whether you're interested in figure drawing, cartooning, or digital art, there's a YouTube channel out there for you.

## 3. **Ctrl+Paint**
[Ctrl+Paint](https://www.ctrlpaint.com/) is a haven for those looking to dive into digital art. While it does focus on digital painting, many of the fundamental drawing lessons are applicable to traditional art as well. With a library of over 200 free videos, you'll have plenty of material to hone your skills.

**Conclusion**  
Drawing is a journey, and with these free resources, you have all the tools you need to embark on your artistic adventure. So grab your pencils or stylus, and start drawing today!
